Game Jam Projects

  1. Discord Mod Simulator: Sole Programmer

    10 Day Jam - July 2023

    Online Chat Simulating Clicker Game (Team of 6)
    • Developed the chat display system, populating it with personality based usernames, profile pictures, and messages to simulate an authentic chat environment.
    • Implemented an upgrade store with multiple paths providing a variety of effects, featuring physical objects displayed in the player's room.
    • Designed and integrated the stream management system, allowing players to interact with dynamic streams.
    • Created a distraction element that hijacks the camera periodically, adding a challenging element to gameplay.
    • Collaborated with artists and sound designers to integrate artwork, animations, and video elements, enhancing the overall immersion of the game.

  2. CORNered: Sole Programmer

    4 Day Jam - May 2023

    2.5D Shooter with Corn-themed Zombie Elements (Team of 5)
    • Designed and implemented a variety of gameplay elements, including weapons, destructible environment, and enemy AI, to create an engaging and immersive experience.
    • Created a dynamic environment with destructible furniture, walls that could be destroyed and rebuilt, and wandering animals prone to explode.
    • Developed four enemy types, including a popcorn/skull-themed boss with three forms, using Astar Pathfinding Project for variable paths in a destructible environment.
    • Collaborated with artists to implement art assets, animations, and sound effects, enriching the visual and auditory experience of the game.

  3. D'MEO: Gameplay Programmer

    7 Day Jam - April 2023

    First-Person Dungeon Crawler (Team of 4)
    • Developed smooth, adaptable, grid-based movement systems that worked for both dungeon traversal and the combat minigames.
    • Collaborated with the team to ensure smooth integration of art assets, sound effects, and game systems, maintaining consistency and coherence throughout the game.
    • Designed the grid-based combat system with a multitude of enemy attacks, scaling difficulties, and complex patterns.

  2. 20XX MAXX: Gameplay Programmer

    Aug 2018 - Dec 2019

    Local Multiplayer Car-Based Battle Arena (Team of 6)
